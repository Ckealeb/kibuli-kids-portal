About Our School

Our Story and Vision

Learn about our journey, mission, and the values that guide our educational approach at Kibuli Muslim Nursery School.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing educational environment where children develop strong academic foundations and Islamic values, preparing them to be confident, responsible, and compassionate members of society.

We are committed to excellence in education while instilling the principles of faith, knowledge, and character in every child who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To be the leading Muslim nursery school in Uganda, recognized for our balanced approach to education that harmoniously integrates academic excellence with Islamic teachings.

We envision our graduates as future leaders who embody the values of integrity, compassion, and wisdom, making positive contributions to their communities and the wider world.

Our Journey

Our History

A glimpse into our humble beginnings and the journey that has made Kibuli Muslim Nursery School what it is today.

2005

Founding Years

Kibuli Muslim Nursery School was founded by a group of dedicated educators and community leaders who saw the need for quality early childhood education with Islamic values in the Kibuli area of Kampala.

Starting with just two classrooms and 30 students, our school was built on the core principle that every child deserves access to excellent education in a nurturing environment.

2010

Growth and Development

By 2010, our school had grown significantly, expanding to accommodate more students and introduce new programs. We renovated our facilities and added specialized learning spaces.

During this period, we also developed our comprehensive curriculum that balances academic learning with Islamic education, a model that has become our hallmark.

2015

Recognition and Achievement

Our dedication to educational excellence was recognized when we received several awards for our innovative teaching methods and commitment to holistic child development.

We established partnerships with other educational institutions and community organizations to enhance the learning experiences of our students.

2023

Present Day

Today, Kibuli Muslim Nursery School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in our community, serving over 500 students with a team of dedicated teachers and staff.

We continue to evolve and adapt to the changing educational landscape while staying true to our founding principles and commitment to nurturing the next generation of leaders.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Core Values

At Kibuli Muslim Nursery School, our core values form the foundation of everything we do. These principles guide our approach to education, our interactions with students and parents, and our role in the community.

Faith & Spirituality

We nurture a deep connection to Islamic values and teachings, providing children with a strong spiritual foundation.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of our educational provision, constantly improving and evolving.

Respect

We foster an environment of mutual respect, appreciating the dignity and worth of every child and family.

Compassion

We teach and demonstrate kindness, empathy, and care for others, nurturing compassionate hearts.
